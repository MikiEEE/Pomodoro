HI! WELCOME!

You currently don't have any projects stored to do work on.
Follow these instructions and you'll be 'Pomodoro-ly' Productive in no time!

ADDING PROJECTS:
  1. Click on the 'Add Project' tab in header bar.
  2. Enter in a name of your project in the Porject Name Section
    It will appear in your Projects list (where it currently says 'No Projects enqueue'.)
  3. Repeat Steps 1-2 to add more projects.
DELETEING PROJECTS:
  1. Click on the 'Delete Project' tab in the header bar.
  2. Enter in a name of your project in the Project Name Section
    The Project will be deleted from your Projects list
RUNNING THE POMODORO:
  1. Click on the 'Home' tab in the header bar.
  2. To Start the Pomodoro interval click the 'Start Timer' button.
    Your time will be recorded at the end of the interval.
DOWNLOADING YOUR PROGRESS:
  1. Click on the 'Home' tab in the header bar.
  2. Click the button labeled 'Download CSV'.
    You will get a CSV formatted document.
